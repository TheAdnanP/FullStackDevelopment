VANILLA CHOCALATE BITES

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making delicious VANILLA CHOLACATE bites. In this recipe your gonna master the art of making Bits. These bite-sized treats are perfect for satisfying your sweet cravings. So lets get started!!!!

Ingredients

  • Dark or Milk Chocolate - 200g
  • White chocolate - 100g
  • Vanilla extract - 1-2 tbsp
  • Butter or Coconut oil - 2 tbsp
  • Crushed biscuits / cookies or nuts – ½ cup (optional for crunch)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare a tray mold:
    • Line a small tray or silicone moulds with parchment paper (or use ice cube moulds for bite shapes).
  2. Melt the Dark Chocolate layer:
    • Break the dark/milk chocolate into pieces
    • Melt it in a heatproof bowl over hot water (double boiler) or in the microwave (20–30 sec bursts, stir in between).
    • Mix in 1 tbsp butter/coconut oil + a pinch of salt.
    • Pour half into the tray/moulds as the base.
    • Sprinkle crushed biscuits/nuts if using.
    • Chill in fridge for 10 minutes to set slightly.
  3. Make the vanilla layer:
    • Melt the white chocolate the same way.
    • Stir in 1 tbsp butter/coconut oil and 1-2 tbsp vanilla extract.
    • Pour this over the first chocolate layer.
  4. Top Layer (Optional):
    • Pour remaining melted dark/milk chocolate over the vanilla layer for a neat sandwich effect.
    • Smooth with a spoon or spatula.
  5. Chill:
    • Refrigerate for at least 1-2 hours until fully set.
  6. Cut and Serve:
    • Remove the tray and cut into bite-sized squares.
    • Or pop them out of silcon moulds directly
